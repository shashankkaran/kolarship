This a website used for study purpose.
Instructions for regular users
Does this website need any registrations?
Any students should not make any registrations in this website. They need not make any login
for exploring this site.
Where can I find the present scholarships which are active?
In the home page of the website scroll down. You will find the Current Openings. It will
redirect you to a page where currently active scholarship /fellowship present. You can use the url there
for more details.
What are these Central, State, Private Organizations and International Organizations?
These are just the classifications where you can find the categorised scholarship or fellowship.
Central: contains the Scholarships/ fellowships provided by the central government.
State: contains the Scholarships/ fellowships provided by the state government.
Private Organizations: contains the Scholarships/ fellowships provided by different
private organisations.
International Organizations: contains the Scholarships/ fellowships provided by
different international organisations.
What are the other classifications of these scholarships/fellowships?
These scholarships/fellowships are further classified based on their type (scholarships or
fellowships), special category (caste, special ability, women, sports, arts), qualification. Apart from
these we have state category in state level classification.
Do all scholarships/fellowships have URLs?
No. Some scholarships/fellowships dont have URLs. For such scholarships/fellowships we
have provided with their contact details. So please contact them.
In case if any URL dont work, please search the name of the scholarship/fellowship. The first
link will be its official link.
Instructions for authenticated users
What is the process of registration?
1. On the top right of the home page, you can find the registration option.
2. Enter every detail asked for and click on register.
3. You will be redirected login page, but before login you will be sent a registration link to your
registered mail id.
4. Click on the link sent to your mail to activate your account.
Note: Make sure you always use your official mail for this website.
What is the login process?
1. If you are a new user, first of all make your registration, and then make a login.
2. If you are old user, then just give your credentials and login.
How to update the password OR What if I forget my password?
1. On the login section you will find the Forget Password click here select that.
2. Now enter your email id through which you always make a login (Official mail id) and click
on Send e-mail.
3. A reset link will be sent to your official mail id.
4. Go to your mail and click on the link to reset your password.
5. Now enter your new password twice and conform your password.
Note: Make sure you use strong password otherwise it will not take into consideration.
How to add new entries into the existing data?
1. Once you are logged in you are redirected to a page where you can add the new data or delete
the existing one.
2. For making the new entries, you just need to follow the format of the existing data. The format
will be displayed with an example in the same page.
3. Based on the example make entries.
How to delete the existing data?
1. Once you are logged in you are redirected to a page where you can add the new data or delete
the existing one.
2. Just enter the name of the scholarship/fellowship you want to delete and click on delete.
Note: This option will delete the permanently. So, make sure, you dont make any unnecessary
deletions.
